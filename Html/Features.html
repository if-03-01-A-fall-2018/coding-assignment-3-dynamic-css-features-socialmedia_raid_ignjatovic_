Why do we need social media?

-School

Social media is a helping tool for all students in many different countries. You can use them to share your homework or to share your projects. Many schools like to use social media because its a helping tool. Teachers can use them to send homework, exercises or to share the grades of the students. Students also like the features of social media because they are always up to date.

-Work

A lot of people use social media in work. Its very helpful because you can stay in contact with one of your Employees or with your boss. You can also share documents or any other important stuff with the help of social media. It is also safe to share files or documents with your co-workers because there is a high security behind the application or websites.

-Marketing

One of the best reasons for your small business to be marketing through social media is that your customers are spending time on these channels. According to Statista, 70% of the U.S. population has at least one social media profile. And by 2018, the number of worldwide social media users is expected to reach about 2.5 billion people. With so many consumers using social media every day, this presents a great opportunity for small businesses who want to reach their online audience.
